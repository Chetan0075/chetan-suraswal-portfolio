01

Workflow Optimization & Delay Prediction

Identifies inefficiencies in task allocation, resource usage, and deadline adherence in project-driven environments. Predicts delay probability before it impacts delivery.

Data Sources → SQL Warehouse → Feature Engineering → Analysis → Power BI → Insights

Key Business Insights

  • Experience alone does not guarantee faster delivery
  • 18% of tasks contribute to over 40% of total delay
  • Resource reassignment timing heavily impacts outcomes

Decisions Enabled

  • Prioritized high-risk tasks for senior resources
  • Flagged tasks exceeding 120% estimated effort
  • Rebalanced workload during peak delivery weeks
  • Delayed non-critical tasks to protect deadlines

02

Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis

End-to-end sales analytics system focusing on revenue trends, profit leakage, and category-level performance across regions and time periods.

Raw Sales Data → Data Cleaning → Profit Modeling → Category Analysis → Revenue Insights

Key Business Insights

  • High-discount orders erode margins without improving volume
  • 18% of SKUs generate 60%+ of total profit
  • Category-level loss concentrated in specific regions

Data Quality & Assumptions

  • Dataset sourced from Kaggle (retail sales domain)
  • Profit calculated post-discount and shipping
  • Returned orders excluded from profit metrics
  • Regional demand seasonality not fully captured
